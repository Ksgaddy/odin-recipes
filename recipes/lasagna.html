Lasagna

Description

This is a recipe for lasagna. Lasagna is like a layered cake, but pasta. It has cheese, sauce, meat, and pasta. It is very delicious! Yum!

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Pasta
  • Ground Beef
  • Pasta Sauce

Steps

  1. Put a layer of pasta in the pan
  2. Place a layer of pasta sauce on top
  3. Place a layer of ground beef on next
  4. Sprinkle on a layer of your cheese of choice
  5. Repeat steps 1-4 until pan is full
  6. Place in unven and cook at a certain temperature for a certaina mount of time
