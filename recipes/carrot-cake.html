Carrot Cake

Description

Carrot Cake is kind of like a spice cake, but with carrots. They typically contain nuts as well as a cream cheese frosting. They are God's gift to man

Ingredients

  • Carrots
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Baking Soda
  • Spices
  • Chopped walnuts

Steps

  1. Whisk flour, baking soda, and oil
  2. Add eggs one at a time
  3. Add dry ingredients
  4. Stir in the carrots and nuts
  5. Place batter in pan and bake for 35-45min
