Italian Cream Soda

Description

Italian cream soda is like drinking a little piece of heaven. Imagine to prickly carbonation of a soda with the creamy thickness of a extremely blended smoothie. Mix it with some sugary flavor and you have the recipe for a delicious summer time treat.

Ingredients

  • Ice
  • Club Soda
  • Heavy whipping cream
  • Flavored syrup

Steps

  1. Fill glas halfway with ice
  2. Add 1/2 cup of club soda
  3. Pour 1/4 cup of flavored syrrup into glass
  4. Add 2 tbsp of heavy whipping cream
  5. Stir glass until whipping cream is fully mixed in
